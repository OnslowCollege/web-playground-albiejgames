Welcome

Hello! I'm RADOTHEGOAT, an aspiring YouTuber born in Papua New Guinea. My dream is to grow from humble beginnings into a global creator who inspires others to follow their passions—no matter where they come from.

Why I Do This

Growing up in a remote part of Papua New Guinea, I didn’t have many resources or examples of creators. But I always believed that with creativity, determination, and hard work, I could build something meaningful. My YouTube channel is more than just videos—it’s a journey.

Through my content, I share my daily life, my struggles, and my small triumphs. I want viewers around the world to not just watch me, but connect, resonate, and feel empowered to chase their own dreams.

Milestones

2018 – First Uploads

The very first videos went up: simple vlogs, stories, and ideas.

2019 – Building Confidence

I started building a small but loyal audience. Their feedback pushed me forward.

2021 – 1,000 Subscribers

A big win. Proof that my journey was beginning to take shape.

2023 – Expanding Horizons

More content, more ambition, and bigger dreams to keep growing.
